F-35A "Lightning II"

Name: F-35A "Lightning II"
Role: Stealth Multirole Fighter
Manufacturer: Lockheed Martin
National Origin: United States
Unit Cost: $94,600,000

General Characteristics
Crew: Pilot
Length: 15.67 meters
Wingspan: 10.7 meters
Maximum Speed: Mach 1.6


The F-35A "Lightning II" is an all-weather stealth multirole fighter. Designed in the hopes of being the final say in every engagement, the F-35 was built to excel. The F-35A was designed for general use, being the only F-35 equipped with a cannon while lacking the STOVL capabilities of the F-35B and the ability to launch from carriers of the F-35C. All versions of the F-35 including the F-35A are equipped with state-of-the-art technology including full panel glass cockpit touchscreens, the latest advances in radar-absorbent-materials, active electronically scanned array radar, and an advanced pilot helmet mounted display system.

The F-35's main strength is its highly advanced avionics suite. The F-35 is capable to tracking and engaging targets no matter their position relative to the aircraft. The F-35 is even capable of engaging targets that are not in front of the aircraft by simply gathering enough information for the missile that the difference in direction is not a problem.

Armament: 25mm GAU-22/A cannon, 6 hardpoints with total capacity of 6800 kg, 2 internal weapons bays with total capacity of 1,360 kg
